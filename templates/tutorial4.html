Let's learn some new words so you can hold up a basic conversation
Here is a conversation between two friends
Attempt the Quiz at the end to proceed to next Stage.




--Two friends Sarah & David--

[Hello]   [How]
Sarah: Hello! How are You?
(Sign Hello and How then point to person for You)

[Good]    [watch]     [movie]     [?]
David: Good! Want to watch a movie?

[No]    [time]     [now]     [sorry]
Sarah: No time right now, sorry.

[Okay]    [next]    [week]
David: Okay! Next week then?

[Yes]
Sarah: Yes!

[Great]     [Good Bye]
David: Great! Good bye.

[Good Bye]   
Sarah: Good Bye!
