Book

To sign book, put your hands together, palm-to-palm. Then keeping your pinkies together, open up your hands as if opening up a book.

For when u want a Book

Bathroom

Bathroom is signed by making letter 'T' with our dominant hand, and then shaking it back and forth in front of us.

It is very useful everyone need a bathroom break!!

Come

Come starts with both your hands extending with index fingers pointing forward. Then pull your arms with index finger still pointing, toward your body.

You can use this gesture to call your friends/teachers towards you.

Count

To sign count , your one hand should be flat with palm facing upwards. Then take your thumb and index finger of the other arm together and make an imaginary circle on your base palm.

Will be helpful in maths class :)

Don't Know

Point to your forehead with your dominant hand with fingers together and move your hand away from you, while slightly shaking your head to show negation. Alternatively, shrug your shoulders.

Forgot

Forget is signed by swiping your dominant hand across your forehead.

Like when u forget your homework at home :(

Friend

Make a letter C with your index fingers and make them hug eachother from both sides like friends do!!

Get Dressed

With open hands, brush down the front of your chest with your thumbs and then away from your body.

Gotta get dressed to go out now !!

Good Morning

Good to learn the basic salutaions for greeting people at school!!

Help

To sign help, place your closed-fist, dominant 'A' hand on top of your non-dominant open palm, and move both hands upwards.

Dont ever be afraid to ask for help!

Learn

Lift your palm up, then spread your other hand's curved fingers on your other palm, then pull your hand up with fingers forming a flat 'O' shape, touching them to your forehead. It is as if you are transferring knowledge from book to your brain.

Question

Simply take your index finger and trace a big question mark in the air in front of you.

You are going to school ofcourse you'll have questions or doubts

Read

To sign "Read" make a flat hand with your non-dominant hand, and with your dominant hand in a 'V' shape, pretend your fingers are scanning a book on your open palm.

Remember

Form both hands into a loose ASL letter A sign. Hold your non-dominant 'A' hand steady before, take your dominant 'A' hand's thumb to your forehead, then bring it down to touch your other thumb.

Remember this sign!!

School

School is signed by clapping your flat, open hands together, with the top hand's fingers perpendicularly angled to the bottom's.

Gotta be ready for it!

Student

To sign "student," first sign "learn" by spreading your dominant hand's fingers on your non-dominant palm, then pull your hand up to your forehead, and finish by sliding both flat hands downward in front of your body for the person.

This sign is useful when you want to ask someone if they are a student or when introducing yourself.

Study

To sign "study," hold your non-dominant hand flat and facing you, then use your dominant hand in a '5' shape to wiggle your fingers in front of it while moving back and forth a couple of times.

Imagine this sign as mimicking your eyes intently reading and studying what is on the page or book before you.

Teacher

To sign "teacher," sign "teach" by closing your fingers and thumbs near your forehead, then sign "person" by bringing parallel open palms down from shoulder to waist level.

Useful when u want to ask for your teacher at school.

test

To sign test, form both hands into hooks (or ASL letter X) Then bring down both 'X' hands as you spread them flat side by side, to signify paper.

Part of your life cannot run from it... All the Best!!

Uniform

first, sign clothes, then sign same, but with both hands making a circle in unison, indicating everyone else in the group.
